                        Problem Statement

 Today, rising  stress , mental health challenges , and lifestyle-related diseases are affecting people across all age  groups.​

 Due to  lack  of early health monitoring , awareness ,and affordable preventive care , health issues are  detected at a later stage.​

 Late detection leads to higher medical costs , reduced productivity, and poor overall wellbeing.​

 Most existing healthcare platforms focus on data collection but fail to provide clear actionable ,and personalized health guidance.​
